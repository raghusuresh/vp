He is regarded as one of the greatest batsman in the history of cricket.Tendulkar took up cricket at the age of eleven,made his Test match debut on 15 november 1989 against pakistan in Karachi at the age of sixteen, and went on to present Mumbai domestically and India internationally for close twenty-four years. In 2002,halfway through his career, wisden ranked him the second-greatest Test batsman of all time, behind Don Bradman, and the second-greatest ODI batsman of all time, behind Viv Richards.Later in his career, Tendulkar was part of the Indian team that won the 2011 Cricket World Cup,his first win in six World cup appearances for India. He had previously been named "Player of the tournament."

Tendulkar recieved the Arjuna Award in 1994 for his outstanding sportig achievements,the Khel Ratna Award, India's highest sporting honour,in 1997, and the Padma Shri and Padma Vibhushanawards. In 2012, Tendulkar was nominated to the Rajya Sabha,the upper house of the Parliament of INdia.In 2010, Time magazine included Tendulkar in its annual time 100 list as one of the most influential people in the world.

Tendulkar was awarded the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for crickekter of the year at the 2010 ICC Awards.Having retired from ODI cricket in 2012, he retired from all forms of cricket in November 2013 after playing his 200th Test match. Tendulkar played 664 internationl cricket matches in total,scoring 34,357 runs.
